Type 001A First Domestic Aircraft Carrier
Type 001A aircraft carrier is the first domestic aircraft carrier built independently in China. Based on the research of Kuznetsov class aircraft carrier of the former Soviet Union and Liaoning class aircraft carrier of China, it has been improved and developed by China itself. It is the first domestic aircraft carrier in the true sense of China.

China Space Station

China Manned Space Station (CSS), or CSS for short, is a space laboratory system with Chinese characteristics assembled on orbit. The construction plan is expected to take place between 2020 and 2025, and is expected to be completed around 2022. The orbital altitude of the space station is 400-450 km, inclination angle is 42-43 degrees, design life is 10 years, long-term residence of 3 people, total weight can reach 90 tons, for large-scale space applications.

China Lunar Exploration Project

China's manned lunar landing project plans to send more than a dozen tons of spacecraft to Earth orbit around 2014 in the field of deep space exploration, and build its own space station by 2020. In terms of lunar exploration, the "return" mission will be realized by 2020, that is, the spacecraft will not only land on the moon, but also take some things back to the earth, and the astronauts will land on the moon around 2030.

Jiaolong manned submarine

Jiaolong manned submersible is a manned submersible designed and developed by China independently and integratedly. It is also a major research project in 863 project. From May to July 2010, Jiaolong manned submersible carried out several dives in the South China Sea, with a maximum depth of 7020 meters.

500 metre spherical radio telescope

The 500-metre spherical radio telescope, known as the "Chinese sky eye", was conceived by Chinese astronomer Nan Rendong in 1994. It took 22 years to complete and opened on September 25, 2016. It is led by the National Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. It has our own intellectual property rights and the largest single-aperture and most sensitive radio telescope in the world.

The 70th Anniversary Parade of the Chinese Navy

China held a naval parade on the 70th anniversary of the founding of the People's Navy in Qingdao and its adjacent sea and airspace, with 32 naval vessels and 39 military aircraft on display. Xi Jinping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, President of the State and Chairman of the Central Military Commission, attended a naval parade in Qingdao on the 23rd to celebrate the 70th anniversary of the founding of the People's Navy.
